Van Specs & exterior

Here, we will talk about the technical specifications of the van, and what changes there will be on the exterior of the van.

The Van

The van is a 2010 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter Cargo van with a 170" extended wheelbase and a High roof. This means that the van came with no windows behind the cabin on the sides, and a high roof means that the interior of the van is extended from a height of 68.7 inches to a height of 79.1, giving an increase of 10.4 inches in height for the interior of the van. The sprinter lineup has a few different wheelbase lengths, being 144", 170", and 170" extended. This transfers to 19.4 feet, 22.8 feet, and 24.2 feet wheelspan respectively. The van being converted is using the 170" extended wheelbase, which is the biggest because the wheels are far appart, like the 170" wheelbase, but also has more added to the back of the van to extend the frame for the longest wheelbase available.
