The van that got me thinking

Hello, my name is D. I. von Briesen. I live in Charlotte NC, and I am starting a van conversion. I first thought about van conversions after seeing a guy with a van in my neighborhood right after getting home from a road trip. I asked him about his van, and talked about buying it, but decided to buy my own van after some research on it, and learning that van was a water damage rebuild. This is a website documenting my plans for my 2010 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter Van I bought nicknamed Kismet. Follow my Journy of converting Kismet from a cargo van into an off the grid camper!
