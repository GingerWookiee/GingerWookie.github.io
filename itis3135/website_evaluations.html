Website Evaluations

Good site: UNCC Academics Page

The home site for the different academic avenues on the UNCC page.

  • C: Words stand out from the page, and parts of the page stand out from one another
  • R: Consistant design with the same font and colors used through the entire page
  • A: A nice gid pattern keeps everything in line
  • P: The page is very clearly organized by subject


Bad site: Arngren

  • C: There is contrast, although overlap of the contrast counteracts/nullifies it to a degerr
  • R: Everything is in boxes, but those boxes have no consistancy to them
  • A: None whatsoever, everything is just thrown where ever
  • P: None, things are thrown where they fit, not grouped together
